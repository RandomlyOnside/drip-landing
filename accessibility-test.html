Accessibility Notes

  • Primary on Secondary: High contrast ratio, excellent readability
  • Accent1 on White: Good contrast for buttons
  • Accent2 on White: Good contrast for buttons
  • Text Opacity: All opacity levels maintain sufficient contrast
  • Font Size: Responsive scaling from 16px base to larger sizes
  • Focus States: Clear focus indicators with ring styles
  • Semantic HTML: Proper heading hierarchy (h1 > h2 > h3)
  • ARIA Labels: Screen reader support for interactive elements
